About Us

The CIS Club is the heart of the Computing & Information System department, bridging the gap between academic learning and professional success.

Our Mission

To foster a collaborative environment where students can enhance their technical skills, network with industry professionals, and prepare for successful careers in technology. We believe in learning by doing and supporting one another.

Our History

Founded in 2023 by a group of five passionate students, the CIS Club has grown from a small study group to the largest departmental organization. Over the past decade, we have hosted over 50 hackathons, 200 workshops, and helped hundreds of students land internships.
